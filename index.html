Semena Center

You defend
human rights. We defend you.

We are Semena Center — a protection hub for human rights defenders facing threats, unlawful arrests, violence, and persecution. Emergency relocation, legal aid, rapid response, mental health, and advocacy: built around the real, urgent needs of those who risk everything for justice.

Emergency Support

Emergency Protection
& Relocation Support

When your safety is under threat, every hour matters. We provide immediate protection, safe shelter, and coordinated relocation for human rights defenders facing violence, arbitrary arrest, or persecution — ensuring you can continue your work without fear.

  • Safe shelter and temporary accommodation
  • Emergency transportation and evacuation
  • Coordination with trusted partner networks
  • Food, medical care, and psychosocial support
  • Reintegration support after crisis situations

Safe Shelter

Secure, confidential accommodation for defenders and their families in crisis.

Rapid Evacuation

Emergency transport and coordinated extraction from high-risk situations.

Network Partners

Coordination with trusted local and international protection organizations.

Reintegration

Ongoing recovery and resettlement support after the immediate crisis has passed.

Legal Counseling

Expert guidance on your rights, options, and legal exposure in complex situations.

Documentation

Thorough recording of human rights violations for legal proceedings and advocacy.

Bail Support

Assistance securing release for unlawfully arrested activists and defenders.

Fair Trial Advocacy

Monitoring court proceedings and advocating for due process and justice.

Legal Aid & Court Support

Legal Aid &
Court Support

We believe access to justice is non-negotiable. Our legal support connects defenders with experienced lawyers, legal aid providers, and human rights institutions — ensuring no one faces the courtroom alone when their freedom is on the line.

  • Legal counseling and referrals
  • Court representation and bail support
  • Monitoring unlawful detention
  • Human rights violation documentation
  • Filing complaints before relevant authorities
Crisis Intervention

Rapid Response &
Crisis Intervention

Threats don't wait for office hours. Our 24/7 rapid response system is built to verify incidents, mobilize support, and coordinate protective interventions for defenders facing immediate danger — within hours, not days.

  • 24/7 incident monitoring and verification
  • Emergency support and referral mobilization
  • Rescue and protection coordination
  • Engagement with authorities when necessary
  • Crisis counseling and emotional support

Trauma Support

Trauma-informed counseling and referrals for defenders exposed to violence and fear.

Peer Support

Community healing spaces where defenders support one another through shared experience.

Wellness Programs

Awareness and resilience programs to support mental health in the long term.

Crisis Care

Immediate emotional support and stabilization during and after acute crises.

Wellbeing & Recovery

Mental Health &
Psychosocial Support

Human rights work takes a profound personal toll. Our organization recognizes that burnout, trauma, and fear are occupational realities — and we are here to ensure defenders have the space, support, and care they need to heal and continue.

  • Trauma-informed support and counseling referrals
  • Peer support and community healing spaces
  • Mental wellness awareness programs
  • Emotional support during crisis
  • Rehabilitation for survivors of violence
Advocacy & Solidarity

Advocacy &
Solidarity Networks

Protection is not only reactive — it is structural. We actively advocate for stronger laws, policies, and mechanisms that protect civic space and human rights defenders, while building solidarity networks that amplify their voices globally.

  • Promoting recognition of defenders' work
  • Raising awareness about attacks and violations
  • Opposing criminalization of activism
  • Accountability for perpetrators of abuse
  • Freedom of expression and peaceful assembly
