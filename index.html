Who We Are

A Shield for Those
Who Protect Others

Semena Center is committed to the protection, safety, and well-being of human rights defenders who face threats, harassment, unlawful arrests, and persecution. We recognise the irreplaceable role that activists, journalists, and civil society actors play in advancing democracy, accountability, and human dignity.


Through emergency response, legal support, psychosocial care, and sustained advocacy, we stand beside those who cannot stand alone.
