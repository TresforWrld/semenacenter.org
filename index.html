Who We Are

A Shield for Those Who Protect Others

Our organization is deeply committed to the protection, safety, and well-being of human rights defenders who face threats, harassment, unlawful arrests, violence, intimidation, discrimination, and persecution because of their advocacy work.

We recognize the important role that activists, community leaders, journalists, social justice advocates, and civil society actors play in promoting democracy, accountability, equality, and human dignity within society.
