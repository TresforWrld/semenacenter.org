What We Do

Our Programs

Due to the increasing risks faced by human rights defenders, especially those working with vulnerable and marginalized communities, the organization has established protection and emergency response mechanisms aimed at ensuring their safety, freedom, and continued ability to carry out their work without fear.

01

Emergency Protection & Relocation

The organization provides emergency support and temporary relocation assistance for human rights defenders who are at immediate risk of violence, arbitrary arrest, persecution, threats, or attacks.

  • Safe shelter and temporary accommodation
  • Emergency transportation and evacuation support
  • Coordination with trusted partners and protection networks
  • Basic necessities including food, medical care, and psychosocial support
  • Reintegration and recovery support after crisis situations
Request Support
02

Legal Aid & Court Support

Our organization supports human rights defenders and victims of abuse through legal assistance and referrals. We collaborate with lawyers, legal aid providers, and human rights institutions to ensure access to justice and fair legal representation.

  • Legal counseling and guidance
  • Court representation and legal referrals
  • Bail and bond support for unlawfully arrested activists
  • Documentation of human rights violations and abuses
  • Advocacy for fair trials and due process
Learn More
03

Rapid Response & Crisis Intervention

We maintain rapid response mechanisms to address urgent cases involving threats, arrests, violence, forced displacement, harassment, or attacks against human rights defenders and vulnerable individuals.

  • Monitor and verify incidents of abuse
  • Mobilize emergency support and referrals
  • Coordinate rescue and protection interventions
  • Engage relevant stakeholders and authorities when necessary
  • Provide crisis counseling and emotional support
