Our Legal Support

Access to Justice for Defenders

Our organization supports human rights defenders and victims of abuse through legal assistance and referrals. We collaborate with lawyers, legal aid providers, and human rights institutions to ensure access to justice and fair legal representation.

The organization strongly opposes arbitrary arrests, torture, illegal detention, and abuse of power by state or non-state actors. We advocate for fair trials, due process, and the right of every person to legal representation.

Our legal support services include comprehensive assistance at every stage of the legal process, from initial counseling through court representation and post-trial advocacy. We work to ensure that no defender faces the legal system alone.

Our Services

Legal Support Services

Legal Counseling & Guidance

Professional legal advice and guidance to help defenders understand their rights, options, and the legal processes relevant to their situations.

Court Representation

Legal representation in court proceedings through our network of partner lawyers and legal aid organizations committed to defending human rights.

Bail & Bond Support

Support for unlawfully arrested activists and defenders, including bail and bond assistance to secure their release from detention.

Monitoring & Documentation

Monitoring unlawful detention and court proceedings, and documenting human rights violations and abuses for accountability and advocacy purposes.

Fair Trial Advocacy

Advocacy for fair trials and due process, ensuring that defenders receive just treatment under the law and that their rights are upheld.

Complaints & Petitions

Support in filing complaints and petitions before relevant authorities, helping defenders seek redress and accountability for violations.
