About Semena Center

Our Foundation

Built to Shield Those Who Shield Others

View Programs

Semena Center was established in direct response to the increasing threats faced by human rights defenders — the activists, journalists, community leaders, and civil society actors who stand on the front lines of justice, often at great personal cost.

Our organization recognizes that these defenders cannot be left to face persecution alone. Through coordinated emergency protection, legal aid, mental health support, and international advocacy, we build a shield around those who cannot shield themselves.

We work across local and international networks, coordinating with trusted partners and protection systems to deliver timely, life-changing interventions. Our approach is holistic — we address not only the immediate physical danger but also the legal, psychological, and social dimensions of threat.

Semena Center strongly opposes arbitrary arrests, torture, illegal detention, and abuse of power by state or non-state actors. We advocate for freedom of expression, peaceful assembly, and the right of every person to pursue justice without fear.

"Every defender who is silenced represents a community left without a voice. We exist to ensure that silence is never the final word." — Semena Center Mission Statement

Our Values

What We Stand For

  • Protection Without CompromiseWe stand by defenders at every stage of risk — from early warning to emergency evacuation and recovery.
  • Dignity and RespectEvery person we support is treated with dignity, confidentiality, and cultural sensitivity.
  • Justice and AccountabilityWe document abuses, advocate for fair trials, and hold perpetrators of rights violations accountable.
  • SolidarityWe believe collective action and international networks are essential to protect civic space and human dignity.
  • Holistic CareWe address the physical, legal, psychological, and social needs of every defender we serve.
